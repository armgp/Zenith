About Us

Our team has came together with one goal. To efficiently form a team and to build a Formula Students racing vehicle which we'll be presenting/performing in the Formula Students event and the Formula Bharath event. Participation isn't the only goal for us at the moment. We try hard to incorporate the best components available and with meticulous calculations, we ensure that our vehicle can stride with ease and that we can attain a certain level of success in those competitions.
